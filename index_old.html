Sth about me

I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at University of Houston. I am working with Dr. Rong Zheng in the Wireless Research System Group. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from The University of Natural Sciences, Hochiminh City, Vietnam in 2006 and M.E. in Computer Science from Chonnam National Univeristy, Gwangju, South Korea in 2009.

My reseach interests include Network Management, Anomaly and Intrusion Detection, Network Optimization, Wireless Network Channel Selection.

I am studying the problem of Optimizing Wireless Network Channel Selection and User Monitoring and I'm doing well thanks to Dr. Zheng's wonderful guidance ;-)
